SERVICES

We Offer:

Premium Cuts and fades

the haircut is the foundation of any barbershop worth its salt. Haircuts and fades are staples. Our barbers stay on top of the latest styles and trends for men and boys to anticipate client needs.

Beard and mustache trim and servicing

our facial hair services also include oil treatments, shaping, and conditioning to keep beard and mustache hair clean and vibrant. we also offer hot-towel massages to open up the pores and provide a premium luxury treatment.

Professional braiding

Braids are a hotly-demanded request from clients looking for cornrows, box braids, locs, or more intricate custom styles.

Hair coloring and gray blending

Gray blending provides more balance and body to the natural color and we apply with a permanent dye or temporary stains, depending on how committed the client is to the look.

Straight razor shave

Unlike standard, off-the-rack razors, a straight razor can shave hair closer to the root within the skin, providing a sharper, more defined look without the razor burn. Quality straight razor shaves are hot and show no signs of cooling off!

Waxing and hair removal

Hair removal doesn't have to be limited to shaving. we offer waxing services to clean up unwanted eyebrow, nose, and ear hair. We offer them alongside your regular hair cutting or servicing or as a full-service package.
